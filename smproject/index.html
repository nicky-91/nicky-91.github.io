Kenya School of Preaching


Kisumu Kenya...

The Kenya School of Preaching had its beginning in October 2012. Twenty men started this program under the direction of Charles Ogutu, a graduate from the Andrew Connally School of Preaching in Arusha, Tanzania. He is assisted by Elias Omollo, also a graduate of ACSOP, who serves as the dean of students.

To give you a perspective of the quality of this school and the nature by which it operates, here is a quote from a recent report sent by Charles about their approach: "we are not in the business of pleasing the students, but we stress hard work and are very keen on good grades (No room for joy riders), it is quality learning." Charles Ogutu, Direction of KSOP.

We are thankful to have men like Charles and Elias preparing men to preach the gospel in Western Kenya.

The sponsoring congregation is the Charlotte Ave church of Christ, Rock Hill, SC and the Coordinator is Mike Reese. Mike can be reached at preachermiker@frontier.com
